Geometric progression:
ar^0, ar^1, ar^2, ar^3, ..., ar^(n-1)
nth term: ar^(n-1)
sum: a*((r^(n)-1)/(r-1)) ; r!=1 && r>1

Arithmetic progression:
a, a+d, a+2*d, a+3*d, .....
nth term: a+ ((n-1)*d)
sum: (n/2)*((2*a)+(n-1)*d)
sum(1st and last term is given): (n/2)*(1st term+last term)

lcm(a,b)=(a⋅b)/(gcd(a,b));
lcm(a,b,c)=(a⋅b⋅c⋅gcd(a,b,c))/(gcd(a,b)⋅gcd(a,c)⋅gcd(b,c));
